About me

At this point I am trying to learn Fullstack and do a horizontal career change.

I enjoy traveling and learning from other cultures as well.

I am also super handy and have completely remodeled a home from top to bottom. This was a challenging project. Prior to taking on this project I had done some electrical work and some framing and drywall. When this project was completed, I could claim plumbing, tiling, hardwood flooring, and kitchen cabinet installation work as well as overall design to my skillset.
